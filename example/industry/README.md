# Industry Intelligence

This directory is for documents that describe your broader industry landscape and for digests that track changes over time. Skills use this to ground recommendations in market context and to surface emerging threats and opportunities.

## What to Include

- **Industry landscape** — Overview of your industry: key trends, disruptive forces, market segments, regulatory environment, and strategic implications for your company
- **Monitoring digests** — Periodic summaries of industry developments, generated by `/monitor-industry`, stored in the `digests/` subfolder

## Structure

```text
industry/
├── landscape.md          # Industry landscape overview and strategic topics
└── digests/
    └── 2026-02-15.md     # Industry intelligence digest for a given date
```

## Getting Started

1. Run `/research-industry` to discover relevant industry sources and topics for monitoring. This creates `landscape.md` with strategic topics, key questions, and curated sources.
2. Run `/monitor-industry` periodically to scan your curated sources and generate digests in `digests/`. Each digest categorises findings as Act Now, Discuss, or FYI.

## File Formats

The industry landscape uses YAML frontmatter:

```yaml
---
title: Industry Landscape
industry: Your Industry Name
date_researched: 2026-02-15
confluence_title:
confluence_url:
---
```

Digests use YAML frontmatter with scan metadata:

```yaml
---
title: Industry Intelligence Digest
date: 2026-02-15
topics_scanned: 4
findings_total: 5
findings_act_now: 1
findings_discuss: 2
findings_fyi: 2
---
```

## Why This Matters

When skills read your industry context, they can:

- Identify opportunities and threats driven by broader market shifts
- Ground assumption mapping in real regulatory and competitive dynamics
- Flag when a strategic bet depends on an industry trend continuing or reversing
- Connect competitor moves to wider industry forces
