# Strategic Direction

## Vision

**Empowering predators with reliable, precision-engineered catching solutions — from hardware to software and everything in between.**

## Strategic Pillars

### Pillar 1: Digital Transformation — AnvilOS

Transition from a hardware-first company to a platform company. AnvilOS is the centrepiece — a cloud-native targeting and delivery management system that turns one-time hardware sales into recurring subscription revenue.

**Key initiatives:**

- Migrate top 50 enterprise clients to AnvilOS by end of FY26
- Achieve 65% recurring revenue mix (currently 53%)
- Launch AnvilOS v3.0 with AI-powered predictive targeting
- Integrate real-time weather and terrain data for improved accuracy

### Pillar 2: Reliability Revolution

Address the elephant in the room — Acme products have a reputation for spectacular failures. The "Reliability Revolution" programme aims to reduce product failure rates by 60% over two years.

**Key initiatives:**

- Mandatory quality gates for all Gadget Innovation Lab products
- Post-deployment failure analysis automation
- Customer success programme for high-usage clients (starting with Wile E. Coyote)
- ISO 9001 certification for all manufacturing facilities

### Pillar 3: Market Expansion

Grow beyond the traditional desert predator market into urban pest control, international hunting, and adjacent verticals.

**Key initiatives:**

- Urban Pest Control division launch (targeting $2B TAM)
- European market entry via Elmer Fudd GmbH partnership
- Southeast Asian market assessment
- Adjacent vertical exploration: security, demolition, construction

## Risks to Monitor

- **Coyote Tech disruption** — if autonomous drones achieve reliable targeting, Acme's hardware business is at risk
- **Roadrunner Systems pivot** — their "guaranteed catch zones" product could change market dynamics entirely
- **Regulatory pressure** — OSHA increasingly scrutinising explosive products and rocket-powered equipment
- **Customer concentration** — over-reliance on a small number of high-volume clients
- **Talent retention** — Gadget Innovation Lab engineers being recruited by Coyote Tech
