# Industry Slack Update Templates

## Single Update (Act Now)

```
:red_circle: *Act Now* | *Industry Intel* | {Topic Name} — {Event headline}

*What happened:*
{2-3 sentences describing the development factually. Include specific dates, figures, and entities.}

*So what:*
• {First strategic implication — what this means for our products, compliance, or roadmap}
• {Second implication — be specific about affected teams or initiatives}

*Recommended response:*
• {Specific, actionable recommendation with timeline}
• {Second recommendation if needed}

:page_facing_up: Full digest: `industry/digests/{date}.md`
Source: {primary source URL}
```

## Single Update (Discuss)

```
:large_yellow_circle: *Discuss* | *Industry Intel* | {Topic Name} — {Event headline}

*What happened:*
{2-3 sentences describing the development factually.}

*So what:*
• {Strategic implication — what this could mean and why it warrants discussion}
• {Second implication if needed}

:page_facing_up: Full digest: `industry/digests/{date}.md`
Source: {primary source URL}
```

## Consolidated Update (multiple topics, multiple findings)

Use this format when a single monitoring run surfaces findings across multiple strategic topics.
Only include Act Now and Discuss items — FYI items are silently added to the digest.

```
:satellite: *Industry Intelligence Update* | {date}

---

:red_circle: *Act Now*

*{Topic Name} — {Event headline}*
{2-3 sentence summary}
• *Implication:* {what this means for us}
• *Recommended response:* {specific action}

---

:large_yellow_circle: *Discuss*

*{Topic Name} — {Event headline}*
{1-2 sentence summary}
• *Implication:* {what this could mean}

*{Another Topic} — {Event headline}*
{1-2 sentence summary}
• *Implication:* {what this could mean}

---

:memo: *FYI items added to digest:* {count} findings across {n} topics (no action needed)

Full digest: `industry/digests/{date}.md`
```

## Event Categories

Use these standardised categories in headlines to make updates scannable:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Regulation | New law, policy proposal, compliance deadline, regulatory consultation |
| Market Shift | M&A, market entry/exit, structural change, deregulation |
| Technology | New standard, platform shift, technology maturity milestone |
| Analyst Report | Market sizing, vendor ranking, trend forecast, maturity assessment |
| Conference | Keynote, industry panel, strategic announcement at event |
| Research | Customer behaviour study, adoption data, market survey |
| Standards | New standard published, certification change, interoperability update |
| Investment | Industry-level funding trends, major investment in a market segment |
