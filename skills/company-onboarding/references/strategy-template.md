# Strategy Template

Use this template to structure the company strategy document. Populate from the conversation and any source documents. This captures the company's strategic direction at a high level — not a detailed strategic plan.

## Frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: Company Strategy
confluence_title:
confluence_url:
---
```

## Sections

### Vision

Company or product vision statement. One or two sentences.

### Strategic Pillars

2–4 key strategic priorities. For each pillar:

```markdown
### Pillar {n}: {Name}

{One paragraph describing the strategic intent}

**Key initiatives:**

- {Initiative 1}
- {Initiative 2}
- {Initiative 3}
```
