# OKRs Template

Use this template to structure OKRs. Each objective follows a consistent format. The PM may share OKRs during onboarding or add them later.

## Frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: OKRs
period: {e.g., H1 FY26}
team: {Owning team name}
confluence_title:
confluence_url:
---
```

## Structure per Objective

```markdown
## Objective {n}: {Objective Name}

**Goal:** {One-sentence goal statement}

**Owner:** {Name or role}

**Why it's important:**

- {Strategic rationale — why this matters now}

**Key Results:**

1. {Measurable result 1}
2. {Measurable result 2}
3. {Measurable result 3}

---
```

Repeat for each objective. Typically 3–5 objectives per period.

Each key result should be specific and measurable — a number, percentage, or binary outcome. Avoid vague results like "improve customer satisfaction."
